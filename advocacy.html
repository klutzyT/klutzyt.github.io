Understanding Cloud Storage

Your data, anytime, anywhere

What is Cloud Storage?

Cloud storage is a method of saving digital data to an off-site system maintained by a third party. Rather than storing information on your computer's hard drive or local server, you send it to a remote database via the internet.

Accessibility

Backup & Recovery

Automatic backups reduce the risk of losing important files due to hardware failures.

Why It's Bad

Security Risks

Storing sensitive data online makes it a target for hackers and data breaches.

Dependence on Internet

If you don't have internet access, you may not be able to access your files.
