Why Cloud Storage Can Be Dangerous

What it is, Pros, Cons, and alternatives

What is Cloud Storage?

Cloud storage is a way of saving data over the internet. weather its pictures, important files, or just backups of device data. Examples of cloud storage include but arent limited to, iCloud, Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, Mega, and DropBox

Reasons To Use It

Accessibility

Anything connected to the internet and is capable of downloading a required app or having a required service (which almost any device made after 2007 has)

Backups

When data is stored in the cloud, it is way less likely to be lost due to most likely multiple servers contaning your data, this can also be a data security risk, which i will get into.

Why It's Bad

Security Risks

Storing sensitive data online makes it a target for hackers and data breaches.

Dependence on Internet

If you don't have internet access, you may not be able to access your files.
